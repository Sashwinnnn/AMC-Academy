The Coin Siege: Step-by-Step Breakdown

Problem: If you flip a coin 5 times, how many outcomes yield AT LEAST ONE tail?

1. Calculate Entire Unrestricted Universe (2 options per flip):
    25=2×2×2×2×2=32 total ways

2. Isolate Forbidden Configuration (The complete opposite of "at least one tail" = No Tails / All Heads):
    Only 1 exact way: {H, H, H, H, H}

3. Complete Complement Subtraction:
    32−1=31 valid solutions.

06 // Core Deep-Dive

Walking Through The Row Math

Problem: 5 items in a row, but 2 items cannot sit side-by-side.

1. Find Total Unrestricted Rows (Normal Factorial):
    5!=5×4×3×2×1=120

2. Find Forbidden Arrangements (Glue the 2 items into 1 block, leaving 4 total objects):
    4!×2!=24×2=48

3. Final Evaluation (Subtract the Clashes):
    120−48=72
