01 // Mathematical Frameworks

We don't rely on raw formulas. We look for patterns.

The AMC 8 doesn't just test how fast you can calculate; it rewards deep problem comprehension. We show students how to break down intricate wording into elegant problem-solving paths.

Strategic Symmetry

Many complex counting and geometry problems contain hidden balance. By recognizing uniform structures early, you can solve half the problem before you write anything down.

Isolating Modular Cycles

Number theory problems frequently rely on repeating patterns. We teach students to confidently map small repeating cycles to quickly find solutions in massive number sets.

02 // Syllabus Archive

Interactive Training Modules

Explore our active lesson segments. These modules are unlocked and updated here right after each classroom run.

W01 // The Backdoor Strategy: Counting the Opposite

Combinatorics & Probability Foundations

If a problem asks you to count dozens of messy possibilities, don't do it the hard way. Instead, look at the complement. Count what you DON'T want, then subtract. This single shift in perspective saves you 2+ minutes per problem.

W02 // Lineups & Lockouts: Managing Tight Constraints

Permutations & Spatial Sequences

Arranging people or items in a row gets confusing when rules pop up. We will look at how to map out seating plans visually, glue restricted items together, and dodge forbidden patterns using subtraction.

W03 // Grid Pathways & Overlaps: Mapping Matrix Fields

Permutations with Repetition & Checkpoint Arrays

Street map grids and coordinate trajectories are classic test items. We will drill the math equations for handling pathways containing duplicate letter strings, routing through forced checkpoints, and resolving blocked streets.

W04 // The Overlap Map: Untangling Complex Systems

Multi-Set Visual Logic & PIE

When different groups share members, normal adding causes mistakes. We will use visual maps to break these challenges down into pieces you can manage, even when three or four sets are overlapping at once.

W05 // DNA of an Integer: Prime Decomposition Hacks

Number Theory & Divisor Architecture

Stop making long, exhausting lists to find the factors of a number. We are going to break integers down into their prime "building blocks," then use that structure to instantly determine divisors, counts, and special properties.

W06 // Clockwork Math: Cracking Modular Cycles

Advanced Remainder & Exponent Mechanics

How do you find the last digit of a giant number like 7^2026 without multiplying forever? You find the heartbeat—the repeating cycle—and use modular arithmetic as your speedboat through impossible-looking exponents.

W07 // Rhythm & Divisibility: Synchronizing Chaos

LCM Frameworks & Fast Filters

When clocks, alarms, or racing cars start at different times, finding out when they line up seems chaotic. We'll harness LCM (Least Common Multiple) as your synchronization engine to solve these problems instantly.

W08 // Mirror Patterns: Rapid Arithmetic Summations

Symmetrical Progressions & Linear Balance

Adding up a massive string of numbers one by one is a trap designed to waste your time. We will use beautiful symmetry principles to collapse huge sequences into simple one-step calculations.

W09 // Relative Speed: Closing the Gap

Dynamic Velocity Systems & Chases

Instead of stressed-out tracking of two different moving objects at the same time, just freeze one of them in your mind. Transform the problem into a single-reference frame and solve it instantly.

W10 // Finding the Anchor: Invariants in Motion

Algebraic Balance & Ratio Locking

When things are changing fast—water evaporating from a pool, ingredients mixing, or groups swapping members—some quantities stay locked in place. Identify that anchor and the whole problem unfolds.

W11 // The Boxing Method: Slicing Irregular Geometry

Boundary Framing & Proportional Scaling

Don't panic when you see a strange, jagged, irregular shape on the test. If you don't know a formula for it, just box it in and work piece by piece. Decomposition is your silent killer move.

W12 // Game Day Mindset: The Triage Blueprint

Advanced Competitive Stress Protocols

Knowing the math doesn't matter if you run out of time or freeze up under pressure. In our final week, we learn how to think like a pilot in turbulence: triage ruthlessly, execute precisely, never panic.
