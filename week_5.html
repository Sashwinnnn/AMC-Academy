04 // Core Tactical Theory

The Divisor Counting Blueprint

If an integer factorizes cleanly into form: N=pa×qb×rc

1. Isolate the exponents of each unique prime building block: a,b,c

2. Add exactly 1 to each exponent to account for the choice of using the 0th power.

3. Total Count Formula:
    Total Divisors=(a+1)×(b+1)×(c+1)
